Asteroids!

The aim of the game is to last as long as you can without hitting an asteroid and getting destroyed.
Over time more asteroids will start to appear, making them harder to avoid.

Controls:
Movement - WASD or Arrow Keys
Shoot - Spacebar
Slow down - Hold Left Shift
Quit - Escape

Asteroids:
Light - These can be destroyed by shooting at them
Dark - These can not be destroyed so be careful to avoid them
